What Is FractylDev?

FractylDev is a application development company for iOS and Android, based in Parker, Colorado. Our mission is to create unique, advanced applications that add functionality and entertainment to smartphone user’s day to day lives. Our apps are designed with powerful functionality, elegant design, and simplicity for the user’s benefit.

FractylDev was founded by six developers, designers, and marketers who want to create products for Smartphone Devices, and to increase their skills as programmers, business owners, and speakers.

The Morse App


At Fractyl Dev, we take pride in our work. We try everyday to create high quality apps. We currently work on our own apps, but we plan to soon work for other clients to produce their own apps, for business, functionality, entertainment, or anything else.

Our first app, Morse, is on the Google Play story right now! Morse vibrates your text messages in Morse Code, so you don’t have to take your phone out of your pocket! It can also teach or quiz you on your Morse Code skills, and send messages in dots and dashes!
