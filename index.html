What is FractylDev?

FractylDev is a software, application, and website development company, based in Colorado. Our mission is to create uniquely functional and beautifully designed products for both the general population and clients.


FractylDev was founded by six intermediate and advanced programmers, designers, and developers who wanted to create unique and powerful applications for Android and iOS users, and to better their skills with out of the box thinking and apps.

Examples of Our Work

At FractylDev, we take pride in making quality, outstanding products. Part of that means working both on our own and with clients and their needs to develop amazing products!


Our first app, Morse, is now available for Android Devices, with iOS support coming soon! It allows you to vibrate your messages in Morse Code, so you don't even have to take it out of your pocket! Not only that, but it also allows you to learn Morse code in a fun learning section, and send messages in dits and dashes to your friends!
