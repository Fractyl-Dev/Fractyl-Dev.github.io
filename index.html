What is FractylDev?

FractylDev is a software, application, and website development company, based in Colorado. Our mission is to create uniquely functional and beautifully designed products for both the general population and clients.


FractylDev was founded by six intermediate and advanced programmers, designers, developers, and most importantly, people.
