What is Fractyl Development?

Fractyl Development is a software, application, and website development company, based in Colorado. Our mission is to create uniquely functional and beautifully designed products for both the general population and clients.

Fractyl Development was founded by six high intermediate and advanced programmers, designers, developers, and most importantly, people.
