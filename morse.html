The Morse App

In a time where the complexity of technologies is ever-increasing, much merit can still be found in the adaptation of old methods to new circumstances.

Morse does just this.

By adapting the language used on the now obsolete telegraph, Morse can provide users unprecedented functionality.

With this pioneering application, users can learn Morse Code, send text messages, and receive text messages (via vibration feedback). From technicians needing a hands-free communication option or handicapped persons seeking a way to use SMS messaging, MORSE has the potential to impact countless users.

Here are some of the amazing features of Morse!

  • View your SMS messages in the Morse SMS inbox.
  • Text your contacts and automatically translating messages into Morse Code.
  • Translate your messages to Morse Code vibrations
  • Play word games and quiz yourself to practice identification of single characters, four-letter words, or five-letter words in Morse Code.
  • Change the vibrations of messages to include the full message, just the contact name, or only the contact first initial.
  • Change the speed of your vibrations for your skill level!

The Morse App is available on the Google Play Store for all Android devices running Android OS 6.0 and above.
