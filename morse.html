Morse

What is Morse?

Morse is a SMS add on app that allows you to learn, text, and read messages in Morse Code. It uses phone vibrations to tell you what your message says so you don't have to take your phone out of your pocket.


Morse is currently in it's beta phase, but it already has a lot of amazing features. It is currently available for Android deviced running Android 6.0 or later!
