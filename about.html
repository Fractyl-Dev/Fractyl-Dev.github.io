Our Story

FractylDev was created by six friends who loved programming, developing, and designing software, applications, and websites. We all came together to create Morse, for a tech competition at our high school.


While creating Morse, we all realized we have a passion for developing, and so we came together to form FractylDev.


FractylDev focuses on creating amazing products with amazing functionality and beautiful design, that stands out in this world of technology.
