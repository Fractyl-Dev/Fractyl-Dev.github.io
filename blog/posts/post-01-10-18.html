Morse: Official Beta Release

By Hunter Ball
January 10th, 2018


Hello everyone! We at FractylDev are proud to introduce our first app on the Google Play Store, Morse.

Morse allows you to translate and send messages in Morse code, learn and quiz yourself to get better at Morse Code, and vibrate your text messages in Morse Code. Now, you don't even have to take your phone out of your pocket to know what your friends text you!

Right now, Morse is in it's beta stages, but you can get it right now on The Google Play store for Android devices running Android 6.0 or later! The iOS Version will be coming soon, so don't worry iPhone users, you will soon be able to text and read your messages in Morse Code.

A new update to Morse will be available in just a few days, with bug fixes and major UI fixes for many devices, so make sure to get the app!
