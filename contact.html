Contact Us

Whether you have an amazing app idea, or you are a concerned user with feedback, or maybe you just want some tips and advice, FractylDev is always open for contact and conversations! FractylDev was built by people who came together to put their skills to use, and communication is very important to us! If you want to contact us, please do not hesitate to reach out in one of the following ways:

Customer Support

FractylDev is a relatively new company, and although we continue to fix and update our apps to the best of our abilities, sometimes bugs end up going through the development stage into the next update. Although we can usually find these and fix them, sometimes we are not aware of the bug.

If you find a bug, glitch, or problem in one of our apps, please feel free to contact us using our support email!

support@FractylDev.com

General Contact and Inquiries

If you have any ideas, features, or app proposals for us, or if you want to work with us, or you just want more information about anything we do or create, feel free to contact us using our general contact and inquiries email.

contact@fractylDev.com

Press Contact

If you want to contact us about writing a story about FractylDev, want to promote our apps, or want to work with us, please contact us using our press email.

press@FractylDev.com
