Waypoint Communication

Waypoint Communication has been specially crafted for those looking for a new way to communicate with those around them.

Waypoint introduces all sorts of functionality aimed at changing the way you talk to those around you.

  • Post notes anywhere you are!
  • Put relavent pictures, text, links, or drawings!
  • View notes anywhere in the world, or explore those close to you
  • Don't want to forget about a memory? You can save your favorite notes from any user

Waypoint communication will be available soon for all modern iOS devices and will come later to the Google Play Store for android.

For more help, visit the support page.
